Group project process assesment

Introduction

During a recent project for an E-Business module, I completed a group project. In this project we combined several platforms to build a chatbot. Our bot boo was built on DialogueFlow, however much of the web development and user interface was implemented on GitHub. This was one of the most successful group projects teams that I have been apart of. We achieved our goal of a MVP before the deadline, because of this success, I am curious to measure some parts of the process and identfy the strengths in our project. As part of this we prepared an intitial report that featured several plans of our design process. I am also interested to see the work patterns of students, whether the commit times and days can be matched to the students timetables.

Times of Commit

Student days are typically between the hours of 9-5pm. I wanted to look at the times of commits and judge whether or note we were working on the assignment during regular hours, and how often we felt the need to work late into the night to meet deadlines. The graphs shows the dates along the x-axis and the times in 24hrs that we committed at on these dates.

From the graph, we can see that we often worked on the piece during college hours, sinead perferring mornings, while Marta often worked at night. There are a few outliers, the 3am submission is of note, especially given how early in the process it was. It shows commitment from the very begining to meeting deadlines and may be a key reason that this project was delivered on time and to schedule.
